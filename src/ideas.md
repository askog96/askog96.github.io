## Good post template

Use this structure for future posts:

## Summary

What the post helps the reader do.

## Environment

Versions, topology, and assumptions.

## Steps

The commands or configuration.

## Verification

How you know it worked.

## Lessons learned

What you would repeat or do differently next time.
